World's Best Lasagna

 

Making this lasagna requires some effort, but the end result is incredibly gratifying and satiating, making it a worthwhile endeavor!

 

Prep Time: 30 mins.  Cook Time: 2 hrs 30 mins.  Additional Time: 15 mins.  Total Time: 3 hrs 15 mins.  Servings: 12.

Ingredients 

1 pound sweet Italian sausage 

 ¾ pound lean ground beef 

 ½ cup minced onion 

 2 cloves garlic, crushed 

 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes 

 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce 

 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste 

 ½ cup water 

 2 tablespoons white sugar 

 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided 

 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves 

 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste 

 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning 

 ½ teaspoon fennel seeds 

 ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper 

 12 lasagna noodles 

 16 ounces ricotta cheese 

 1 egg 

 ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced 

 ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

 

Directions 

Step 01: 

Gather all your ingredients. 

 

 

Step 02: 

Brown the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven by cooking them over medium heat until well done. 

 

 

 Step 03:

Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water into the mixture, and stir everything together. Sprinkle in 2 tablespoons of parsley, sugar, basil, 1 teaspoon of salt, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, and pepper to season. Cover and let it simmer for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally. 

 

 

Step 04: 

Start by boiling a large pot of water with a pinch of salt. Then, cook the lasagna noodles in the boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. After that, drain the noodles and give them a rinse with cold water. 

 

 

Step 05: 

Incorporate the egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt into the ricotta cheese in a mixing bowl.  

 

 

Step 06: 

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). 

Step 07: 

In order to put it together, start with spreading 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce evenly at the bottom of a baking dish measuring 9x13 inches. Then, proceed to arrange 6 noodles lengthwise on top of the meat sauce. After that, spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture on the noodles. Following this, layer one-third of the mozzarella cheese slices on top of the ricotta cheese mixture. Then, spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce evenly over the mozzarella cheese slices, and lastly, sprinkle 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese on top of the meat sauce. 

 

 

Step 08: 

 Layer the ingredients repeatedly and sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top. Use foil to cover the dish, but ensure the foil does not touch the cheese to prevent sticking. Alternatively, you can spray the foil with cooking spray.

 

 

Step 09: 

Begin baking in the oven after it has been preheated for 25 minutes. After the initial 25 minutes, take off the foil and continue baking for another 25 minutes. 

 

 

Step 10: 

Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
